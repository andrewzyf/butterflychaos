📖 HOW TO PLAY

🎮 The Core Loop

You have one goal: Cause maximum destruction before the 30-second timer runs out. You will draft powers, set up your room in frozen time, and then hit GO to unleash physics-based chaos!

🛠️ Your Arsenal (Items)

  • 🎳 Bowling Ball: Heavy and frictionless. Plows through walls.
  • 🚗 RC Rocket Car: Drives straight and explodes on impact.
  • 🧲 Singularity Magnet: Pulls all nearby metal objects toward it.
  • 🛢️ Unstable Keg: Flies erratically like a balloon before detonating.

✨ Global Modifiers

During the Draft phase, you might pull a modifier card that changes the entire room's rules:

  • 🪐 Micro-Gravity: Objects float gracefully through the air.
  • 🧊 Zero Friction: The floor becomes a slip-and-slide of doom.
  • 💥 Michael Bay Protocol: EVERY single broken object triggers a secondary explosion.

🕹️ Controls

  • 🖱️ Desktop: Drag to orbit · Wheel to zoom · Click a tray item, then click any surface to place it · R rotates · Space = GO.
  • 📱 Touch: Drag to orbit · Pinch to zoom · Tap tray item, then tap a surface.
  • 🎮 Gamepad: Right stick orbits · A places at the crosshair · B picks up · X rotates · Start = GO.

🏆 Winning a Floor

Every smashed plate, exploded keg and shattered monitor scores points. Beat the TARGET to clear the floor and draft a new power. Miss it? Your items stay exactly where you placed them — tweak the setup and try again!
